Spartan Valet

Spartan Valet is designed to be a mobile application. It is a fully functional, responsive web-app with a real database. This app is designed to help a valet runner remember where he or she parked his or her cars for the night. Parts of this application are still in development like the "Tracking" feature that allows the runner to locate a car on a realtime map.
